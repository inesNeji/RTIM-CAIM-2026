About the Conference

RTIM-IMAI 2026 brings together researchers, academics, and industry professionals working in intelligent machines and artificial intelligence. The conference promotes theoretical foundations, real-world applications, and responsible, sustainable AI systems.

Conference Topics

Large Language Models & Generative AI

Architectures, training, evaluation, multimodal LLMs, prompt engineering, responsible AI.

Computer Vision & Visual Intelligence

Image/video analysis, detection, segmentation, vision transformers, multimodal systems.

AI Applications & Intelligent Systems

Healthcare, robotics, IoT, smart cities, finance, security, sustainability.
